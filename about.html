My Story

Hello! My name is Victoria DeSimone, and I am currently a Graphic Information Technology student with a focus on full-stack development at Arizona State University. I strive to combine my passions for design and development to build beautiful, and easy to use websites.

Before enrolling in college in 2025, I spent two years learning Front-End Development through Coursera courses, YouTube videos, and technical documents. This experience gave me the chance to learn and experiment on my own with building simple websites and applications. Whilst creating on my own, I realized that I wanted to expand my learning beyond front-end development, which led me to enrolling in the ASU x Starbucks SCAP program through my employer.

My main focus during my time at ASU is to build upon my previous front-end knowledge to design and make aesthetic, accessible, and functional full-stack multi-page websites.
